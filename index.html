Complex Gorilla Home Page

Complex Gorilla is a VR Game inspired by the famous game known for its unique movements, Gorilla Tag. Complex Gorilla includes many fun things to occupy yourself such as, a trampoline to bounce yourself really high, trees with good routes to branch on, and so much more in the future! There is also cosmetics to try on for you and your friends for FREE! Why don't you give Complex Gorilla a shot and become robot.

This is the Complex Gorilla Home Page. Here you can find message of the day, when is a new update coming out or about a new update, contact info page, and more in the future.
